Ampersand

Ampersand is a quantum physics-inspired music video for the song "In Love with Dusk" by Keep Shelly in Athens. I won a Sloan Science and Film Grant to produce it, and it has screened in film festivals and galleries in almost a dozen countries across five continents.

I'll be releasing Ampersand here at the beginning of February, but if you'd like to see it before then, please email me.
