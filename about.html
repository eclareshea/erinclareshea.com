About

I'm a Denver, CO-based designer and multi-media artist. My animated films have screened in galleries and festivals across five continents, and music and themes of nature, science, and spirituality inspire my work.

I also have a passion for UI/UX/Product Design, and I most recently worked as the first designer at Signal Sciences in Los Angeles.

I received my MFA in Animation from the University of Southern California School of Cinematic Arts where I was an Annenberg Fellow for my work on visualizing information. I also won a Sloan Science and Film Grant to produce my thesis film, Ampersand, a music video inspired by the wave particle duality.
